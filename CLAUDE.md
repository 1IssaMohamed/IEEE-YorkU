## Design Context

### Users
- Primary audience includes students interested in joining or learning about IEEE YorkU.
- Secondary audience includes sponsors and guest speakers evaluating collaboration opportunities.
- Typical jobs to be done in one visit: understand what IEEE YorkU does, assess credibility and culture, discover events/team, and find a clear contact or join path.

### Brand Personality
- 3-word personality: kinetic, fun, resonant.
- Emotional target: professional and trustworthy with visible student curiosity and energy.
- The interface should avoid feeling overly corporate or rigid.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Keep IEEE blue and YorkU red as the core identity colors.
- Keep a single polished light theme.
- Use moderate motion with subtle decoration; motion should support meaning and orientation.
- Maintain the current strong typographic contrast between display and body styles (for example, Sora for headings and Manrope for body copy).
- Anti-direction: avoid generic AI-like styling patterns such as excessive decorative gradients and over-polished visual noise.

### Design Principles
- Lead with clarity and trust: users should understand the branch purpose and value quickly.
- Balance professionalism with student energy: serious where it matters, lively where it helps engagement.
- Keep brand color usage intentional: IEEE blue and YorkU red should signal hierarchy and action, not decoration.
- Accessibility is mandatory: target WCAG AA baseline and push to AAA where practical.
- Motion must be purposeful: subtle transitions that aid comprehension, never distracting ornament.
